# Life Mechanics & Online Tools

This directory contains various interactive tools, simulators, and calculators built to explore trade-offs, model finite resources, and assist with digital workflows.

## 🧭 Life Mechanics Hub

Interactive dashboards designed to visualize, analyze, and balance personal resources and trade-offs.

*   **[The Three Switches (3S)](3S/)**: Balance Time, Money, and Health. The classic life paradox: you can only maximize two at any given stage of life.
*   **[The Four Burners (4B)](4B/)**: Manage 25 energy points across Career, Family, Friends, and Health.
*   **[The Heartbreak Triad (3L)](3L/)**: Balance Self-Awareness, Peace, and Love after heartbreak (only two can coexist).
*   **[The Sleep Paradox (3N)](3N/)**: Balance early sleep, late sleep, and waking life. Explore the economics of sleep debt, revenge bedtime procrastination, and orthosomnia.

## 🛠️ Utility & Productivity Tools

AI-powered applications, text utilities, and gaming arenas.

*   **[Tic-Tac-Toe Arena (TTT)](TTT/)**: Modern neon arena featuring 9 creative game modes and smart AI.
*   **[Humanize — Manuscript Desk (HZ)](HZ/)**: AI-powered text rewriter to restore natural human flow, cadence, and rhythm to stiff, robotic, or AI-generated copy.
*   **[NoteTile (NT)](NT/)**: A fast, offline-first, Progressive Web App for writing and structuring markdown notes.
*   **[VTT Text Extractor Pro (VTT)](VTT/)**: Fetch and clean YouTube subtitles/captions into readable text blocks, with optional AI summarization.
*   **[Transcript Studio for YouTube (YT)](YT/)**: A browser extension and Python CLI to manage, search, translate, and export YouTube transcripts.
