How to Download & Use

Install Anki

Download Anki from apps.ankiweb.net. It's available for Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android.

Download the Deck

Click the download button below to get the flashcard deck (.apkg file). Save it to a location you can easily find.

Import into Anki

Open Anki, click "Import File" at the bottom of the main window, and select the downloaded .apkg file. The deck will be added to your collection automatically.

Start Studying

Click on the deck name to begin studying. Anki will show you cards and ask you to rate how well you remembered them. The app will automatically schedule reviews based on your responses.

Study Daily (Optional but Recommended)

For best results, review your cards daily. Even 10-15 minutes a day can lead to significant long-term retention!
