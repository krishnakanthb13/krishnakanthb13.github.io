⚡ Usage Beyond Limits

If you also have credits on your Zen balance, you can enable the Use balance option in the console.

  • When enabled, Go falls back to your Zen balance after reaching usage limits
  • Instead of blocking requests, it charges against your balance
  • Track current usage in the OpenCode console
  • If limits are reached without balance enabled, you can still use free models

📉 Why Some Models Have Lower Usage

With Go, you pay $10/month and we aim to give you 6x that in usage.

  • Most models: bulk discounts + reserved GPU capacity make the 6x multiplier work
  • Some models haven't had discount opportunities — either new or already priced competitively
  • These models still give you slightly more than paying providers directly
  • That's why their usage multiplier is lower in the table above
