  • Backup Regularly: Ensure NordPass is regularly backed up to prevent data loss.
  • Strong Master Password: Use a strong, unique master password and never share it.
  • Enable 2FA: Turn on two-factor authentication for an added layer of security.
  • Regular Audits: Periodically audit your stored information to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities.
  • Prefer Passkeys: Where a site supports them, use passkeys — phishing-resistant and storable in NordPass — instead of a password + OTP.
  • Watch for Breaches: Use NordPass's Data Breach Scanner / Password Health to catch reused, weak, or leaked credentials early.
  • Stay Updated: Keep NordPass and all your software up to date for the latest security features.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure that your sensitive information remains secure and easily accessible whenever you need it.
