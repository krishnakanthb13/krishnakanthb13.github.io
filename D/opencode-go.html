OpenCode Go

A low-cost subscription for popular open coding models — $5 for your first month, then $10/month. Reliable access to GLM, Kimi, MiMo, MiniMax, Qwen, and DeepSeek through OpenCode.

💰 Price: $5 first month · $10/mo 🌍 Regions: US · EU · Singapore 🔓 Optional: Not required to use OpenCode 🔒 Privacy: Zero-retention, no training
OpenCode Go is designed primarily for international users, with models hosted across three regions for stable global access. Providers follow a zero-retention policy and do not use your data for model training.

How It Works

  1. Sign in to OpenCode Zen, subscribe to Go, and copy your API key.
  2. Run the /connect command in the TUI, select OpenCode Go, and paste your API key.
  3. Run /models in the TUI to see the list of models available through Go.

Only one member per workspace can subscribe to OpenCode Go.

Privacy

Models are hosted in the US, EU, and Singapore. Providers follow a zero-retention policy and do not use your data for model training.

Goals

  • Make AI coding accessible with a low-cost subscription.
  • Provide reliable access to the best open coding models.
  • Curate models that are tested and benchmarked for coding agent use.
  • No lock-in — use any other provider with OpenCode as well.
