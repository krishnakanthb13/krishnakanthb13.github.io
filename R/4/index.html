RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It's a technique that lets AI search your company's documents and knowledge base before generating a response. This means AI answers are grounded in your actual data, not just general knowledge — making them accurate and relevant to your business.

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is a standard that connects AI models to external tools and data sources — databases, CRMs, calendars, APIs, and internal software. It allows AI to take actions and retrieve information from your business systems, making it truly integrated into your workflow.

AI Agents are autonomous systems that can reason through multi-step tasks, make decisions, and execute complex workflows. Unlike simple chatbots, agents can plan, use tools, handle approvals, and manage entire business processes with minimal human oversight.
